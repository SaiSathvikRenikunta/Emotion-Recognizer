Description:


Multimodal Emotion Recognition is a relatively new discipline that aims to include text inputs,
as well as sound and video.This field has been rising with the development of social networks
that gave researchers access to a vast amount of data.Recent studies have been exploring
potential metrics to measure the coherence between emotions from the different channels.

Information:


-->In this project two different inputs audio or video could be given as input.

-->If audio was to be given as input then an local mp3 file is to be uploaded.

-->The system recognizes different emotions that are observed in the audio.
